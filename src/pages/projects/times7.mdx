Times-7 is a global leader in RFID antenna design and manufacturing, serving industries where precision and reliability are critical. As the company prepared for its next stage of growth, it became clear that their existing website was no longer reflecting the scale, innovation, or professionalism of their business.

To position Times-7 alongside the global multinationals they compete and collaborate with, I led a full redesign of their digital presence—reworking the user experience, information architecture, and visual identity. The focus was on creating a site that not only looked the part but also clearly communicated the value Times-7 brings to the market.

The result is a streamlined, modern website that tells the brand’s story of technical excellence, supports future business development, and makes it easier for customers to discover, understand, and access the products they need. Designed for clarity, credibility, and conversion, the new site sets Times-7 up for long-term digital success.
